The Pivot: Why Now?

Retail management is the ultimate masterclass in reactive problem solving.

When a delivery lorry breaks down or three staff members call in sick during Christmas week, you don't form a committee. You reallocate resources, mitigate the risk to service levels and deliver the day.

While I excel at this, I want to change when I solve problems. I am pivoting to project management to focus on proactive delivery—planning the capacity, tracking the milestones and mitigating the risks before they hit the floor.
